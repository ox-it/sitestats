MAVEN 1 INSTALLATION
====================

Manually copy jars to maven repository (adapt paths to your installation):
    1a. mkdir -p ~/.maven/repository/org.ajax4jsf/jars
    1b. cp lib/ajax4jsf-1.1.1.jar ~/.maven/repository/org.ajax4jsf/jars
    
    2a. mkdir -p ~/.maven/repository/org.apache.xmlgraphics/jars
    2b. cp lib/xmlgraphics-commons-1.1.jar ~/.maven/repository/org.apache.xmlgraphics/jars
